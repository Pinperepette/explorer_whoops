Lo scambio Jes Staley ↔ Jeffrey Epstein

9-10 Luglio 2010
Staley: "Maybe they're tracking u?? That was fun. Say hi to Snow White."
Epstein: "[W]hat character would you like next?"
Staley: "Beauty and the Beast"
Epstein: "well one side is available."
La frase "what character would you like next" implica un catalogo di persone identificate con nomi di personaggi Disney. "One side is available" suggerisce disponibilita di una persona.
